Spinner game

Description

In this game you will have a $100, you will pick an amount of money to spin.
Death is the worst, cherry is better, watermelon is the same, bell are even better,
stars are extremly lucky, and the most luck you can get is the special one! To learn more, type help when asking for a bet

You could win, lose, or tie. Once the game is done you could play again.
